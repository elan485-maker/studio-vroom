How the Adoption Process Works

Bringing home your car should be joyful. Here's how we make it easy.

Step 01

Check the Car

Browse our inventory online. Each car has a full profile — name, personality, history, and photos.

Step 02

Contact Us

Found your match? Reach out by email or phone. We'll confirm availability and answer any questions.

↓

Step 03 — Choose what works for you

Option A

Video Chat

We take you on a live tour of the car from wherever you are — no travel needed.

OR
Option B

Visit in Person

Come see the car yourself at our Bensenville location. We love meeting you in person.

↓

Step 04 — Choose how you get the car

Option A

We Deliver

Sit back — we ship your car straight to your door, anywhere in the country.

OR
Option B

Pick Up

Swing by and drive your car home yourself. Nothing beats that first drive feeling.

↓
Step 05

Home

Welcome to the family! Your car found its forever home. Time to make memories together.

About Us

The World's First Car Adoption Agency

Here at Studio Vroom, we love cars — and everything car-related. And we want to share that same joy and appreciation with you.

"To us, cars aren't just transportation — they're like family."

We take care of them and make sure they're clean and healthy. We recognize that they each have their own unique backgrounds and personalities — which is why we give them their own names.

Our expert staff will listen to you to help you identify, locate, and take home the car that will be the best fit — for you.
