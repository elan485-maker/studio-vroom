How the Adoption Process Works

Bringing home your car should be joyful. Here's how we make it easy.

Step 01

Think Your Budget & Ask the Road Shepherd

Tell our Road Shepherd what you're looking for and your budget. We'll listen and help you find the family member that fits you best.

Step 02

Contact Us

Reach out by phone, text, or email. We'll confirm availability and answer any questions you have.

Step 03

We Suggest

We send everything you need to decide with confidence — no guesswork.

📸 Photos 📄 CARFAX Report 🔧 Condition Report

Step 04

Car Inspection

Our team goes over the car inside and out — mechanical, cosmetic, and safety — so every family member is healthy before it goes home.

Step 05

Documentation

We handle the paperwork — title, bill of sale, and registration support — and walk you through every document.

Step 06 — Bring your new family member home

Pick Up

Swing by our Bensenville location and drive your car home yourself.

OR

Delivery

Sit back — we ship your car straight to your door, anywhere in the country.

For Current Owners

Rehome Your Car

Every car deserves a loving next chapter. Whether we buy it from you or find its next family, we make saying goodbye simple and warm.

Step 01

Tell Us About Your Car

Share the details and a few photos. Our Road Shepherd gets to know your car — its story, its condition, everything.

Step 02

Free Appraisal

We inspect the car and check the market — CARFAX, condition, and value — then give you an honest, no-pressure quote.

Step 03 — Choose how you want to rehome

Fast & Simple

Sell to Us

We adopt your car directly with an instant offer. Get paid fast — no listings, no waiting, no strangers.

Best Value

We Sell for You

On consignment, we find the perfect new family — photos, listings, showings, and paperwork all handled by us.

Step 04

Handover & Get Paid

We take care of the title and paperwork, hand things over smoothly, and your car heads off to its next forever home. 🩵

About Us

The World's First Car Adoption Agency

Here at Studio Vroom, we love cars — and everything car-related. And we want to share that same joy and appreciation with you.

“To us, cars aren't just transportation — they're like family.”

We take care of them and make sure they're clean and healthy. We recognize that they each have their own unique backgrounds and personalities — which is why we give them their own names.

Our expert staff will listen to you to help you identify, locate, and take home the car that will be the best fit — for you.
